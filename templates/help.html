How to Use Versify
Your Guide to Formatting Poems

Versify is designed to help you format and organize your poems effortlessly. Follow the steps below to get started:

  • Step 1: Navigate to the "Upload" page by clicking the "Get Started" link below.
  • Step 2: Upload your poem file using the "Upload Poem File" button. Alternatively, you can type or paste your poem content directly into the text area provided.
  • Step 3: Choose how you want to format your poem:
    • If you want all stanzas to have the same number of lines, check the "All stanzas same length" checkbox and enter the number of lines per stanza in the field.
    • If you prefer different stanza lengths, leave the checkbox unchecked and provide a comma-separated list of lengths in the field.
  • Step 4: Click the "Upload" button to process and format your poem. The formatted poem will be displayed on the same page.
  • Step 5: If the formatted poem is available, you can download it by clicking the "Download Formatted Poem" link.
